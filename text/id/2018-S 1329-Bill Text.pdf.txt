SECTION 2. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In accordance with Section 67-3519,
Idaho Code, the Lava Hot Springs Foundation is authorized no more than fif-
teen and eight-tenths (15.80) full-time equivalent positions at any point
during the period July 1, 2018, through June 30, 2019, unless specifically
authorized by the Governor. The Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee
will be notified promptly of any increased positions so authorized.

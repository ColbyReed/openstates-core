A BILL
TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
Relating to the City of Anniston in Calhoun County;
to amend Section 6.07 of Act 404 of the 1953 Regular Session
(Acts 1953, p. 472), now appearing as Section 45-8A-23.176 of
the Code of Alabama 1975, relating to the operation of the
council-manager form of government; to further provide for the
transfer of unencumbered appropriation balances.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
Section 1. Section 6.07 of Act 404 of the 1953
Regular Session (Acts 1953, p. 472), now appearing as Section
45-8A-23.176 of the Code of Alabama 1975, is amended to read
as follows:
"ยง45-8A-23.176.
"The city manager may at any time transfer any
unencumbered appropriation balance or portion thereof between
general classifications of expenditures within an office,
department, board, or agency, subject to subdivision
paragraphs (a) to (j), inclusive, of subsection subdivision
(2) of Section 45-8A-23.091. At the request of the city
manager and within the last three months of the budget year,
the The council may by resolution transfer any unencumbered
appropriation balance or portion thereof from one office,
department, board, or agency, subject to subdivisions
paragraphs (a) to (j), inclusive, of subsection subdivision
(2) of Section 45-8A-23.091. No transfer shall be made from
the appropriations fixed by law or required by subsections
(a), (b), (c), (d), (e), (f), (g), (h), and (i) of Section
45-8A-23.144."
Section 2. This act shall become effective
immediately following its passage and approval by the
Governor, or its otherwise becoming law.

SB289
With Notice and Proof
ENROLLED, An Act,
Relating to Mobile County; to repeal Act 82-675 of
the 1982 First Special Session (Acts 1982 Special Sessions, p.
109), as amended by Act 88-423, 1988 Regular Session (Acts
1988, p. 624), relating to the appointment, compensation, and
funding of employees in the Office of District Attorney of the
13th Judicial Circuit.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
Section 1. Act 82-675 of the 1982 First Special
Session (Acts 1982 Special Sessions, p. 109) as amended by Act
88-423, 1988 Regular Session (Acts 1988, p. 624), relating to
Mobile County and the appointment, compensation, and funding
of employees in the Office of District Attorney of the 13th
Judicial Circuit, is repealed.
Section 2. This act shall become effective on the
first day of the third month following its passage and
approval by the Governor, or its otherwise becoming law.

ENROLLED, House Joint Resolution,
COMMENDING REPRESENTATIVE MICKY HAMMON FOR
PROFESSIONAL SERVICE AS MAJORITY LEADER FOR THE REPUBLICAN
PARTY IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES.
WHEREAS, it is with great pride and pleasure that we
recognize and commend Micky Hammon for his service to the
people of Limestone and Morgan Counties, the Alabama
Legislature, and citizens of the State of Alabama;
Representative Hammon was elected in 2002 to serve in the
Alabama House of Representatives from District 4; in 2010, he
was elected to serve as Majority Leader by the Republican
members of the House of Representatives; and
WHEREAS, Representative Hammon is a graduate of
Calhoun Community College and works as a contractor; he is a
member of the Business Council of Alabama, the Morgan County
Economic Development Association, the Decatur Chamber of
Commerce, the U. S. Chamber of Commerce, the National
Federation of Independent Businesses, the Associated Builders
and Contractors, and the Tennessee Valley Training Center; and
WHEREAS, Representative Hammon and his wife Pam are
the parents of three children, Jake, Colter, and Davis-Anne;
and Representative Hammon is a member of the following
committees: Commerce and Small Business; Economic Development
and Tourism; Limestone County Legislation; and Vice Chair of
Internal Affairs; and
WHEREAS, throughout the years, Representative Hammon
has sponsored and co-sponsored numerous legislative measures,
most notably, an immigration reform bill in 2011 that became
known as the Beason-Hammon Alabama Taxpayer and Citizen
Protection Act, which provided for the enforcement of the
immigration laws in Alabama; and
WHEREAS, with civic-minded ingenuity and purposeful
dedication, Representative Hammon has been guided by a keen
interest in progress and improvement for all citizens; he has
rendered extensive service to members of the House Majority
Party who are indebted to him for his efforts on their behalf;
now therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That in recognition of exemplary
service, leadership, and contributions, we commend
Representative Micky Hammon for his public service as Majority
Leader for the Alabama House of Representatives, and by copy
of this resolution, we extend best wishes for all future
endeavors.

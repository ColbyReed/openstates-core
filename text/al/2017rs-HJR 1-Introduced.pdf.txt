COMMITTEE APPOINTED TO NOTIFY GOVERNOR LEGISLATURE
IS IN SESSION.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That a committee of six, consisting
of three members of the House, to be named by the Speaker of
the House, and three members of the Senate, to be named by the
Presiding Officer of the Senate, be appointed to notify the
Governor that the Legislature is now in session and ready for
the transaction of business.

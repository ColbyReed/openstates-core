RECOGNIZING THE 100TH ANNIVERSARY OF THE ALABAMA
BEVERAGE ASSOCIATION.
WHEREAS, The Alabama Beverage Association is the
trade association for Alabama's refreshing, nonalcoholic
beverage industry, serving the public and its members since
1918; and
WHEREAS, formerly known as the Alabama Soft Drink
Association, the name was changed in 2005 to better reflect
the expanded range of nonalcoholic beverages the industry
provides to Alabama consumers; and
WHEREAS, Alabama's beverage companies manufacture
and distribute some of the most popular nonalcoholic beverages
in the world, which range from refreshing beverages including
100 percent juice and juice drinks; ready-to-drink teas;
sports drinks, bottled water and soft drinks; and
WHEREAS, Alabama's beverage companies play an
important role in the state's economy by providing more than
4,500 jobs, paying significant tax dollars to the state and
federal government and making generous charitable
contributions to organizations across the state; and
WHEREAS, the beverage companies in this state have
made a commitment to improve the health of the community; the
calorie content of beverages being sent to schools has been
lowered by 90 percent in an effort to do their part to address
childhood obesity; now therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, BOTH
HOUSES THEREOF CONCURRING, That we recognize and commemorate
the Alabama Beverage Association for its 100 years of positive
economic and social impact on our state.

A BILL
TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
Relating to the City of Stevenson in Jackson County;
to authorize the governing body of the City of Stevenson to
regulate and permit the sale of alcoholic beverages during
certain hours on Sunday by properly licensed retail licensees
serving the general public in the city.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
Section 1. The city council or other governing body
of the City of Stevenson in Jackson County, by resolution
adopted by a majority of the council, may regulate and permit
the sale of alcoholic beverages during certain hours on Sunday
as determined by the city council by properly licensed retail
licensees of the Alcoholic Beverage Control Board and the City
of Stevenson serving the general public in the city.
Section 2. This act shall become effective
immediately following its passage and approval by the
Governor, or its otherwise becoming law.

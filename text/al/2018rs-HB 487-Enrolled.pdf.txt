ENROLLED, An Act,
Relating to Colbert County; to authorize the county
commission to regulate and permit the sale of alcoholic
beverages during certain hours on Sunday by properly licensed
retail licensees serving the general public in the
unincorporated areas of the county.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
Section 1. This act shall apply only to Colbert
County in the unincorporated areas of the county.
Section 2. The Colbert County Commission may
regulate and permit the sale of alcoholic beverages during
certain hours on Sunday, as determined by the county
commission, by properly licensed retail licensees of the
Alcoholic Beverage Control Board serving the general public in
the unincorporated areas of the county.
Section 3. This act shall become effective
immediately following its passage and approval by the
Governor, or its otherwise becoming law.

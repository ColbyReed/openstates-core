A BILL
TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT
Relating to the City of Priceville in Morgan County;
to authorize the sale of draft or keg beer under certain
conditions.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:
Section 1. This act only applies to the City of
Priceville in Morgan County.
Section 2. The Priceville City Council, by
ordinance, may regulate and permit the sale of draft or keg
beer by properly licensed retail licensees of the Alcoholic
Beverage Control Board for on-premises consumption and for
off-premises consumption provided the beer sold for
off-premises consumption may not exceed 288 ounces per
customer per day which is sealed, labeled, packaged, and taxed
in accordance with state and federal laws and regulations.
Section 3. All laws or parts of laws which conflict
with this act are repealed.
Section 4. This act shall become effective
immediately following its passage and approval by the
Governor, or its otherwise becoming law.

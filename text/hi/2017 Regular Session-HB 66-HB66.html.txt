

     SECTION 1.  Chapter 205, Hawaii Revised
Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part I to be appropriately
designated and to read as follows:

     "ยง205-     Reclassification
and transfer of certain agricultural lands.  (a)  Notwithstanding
any other law, the legislature, by the adoption of a concurrent resolution
approved by a two-thirds vote of each house of the legislature voting
separately, may:

     (1)  Reclassify agricultural land with soil
classified by the land study bureau's detailed land classification as overall
(master) productivity rating class C or D from an agricultural district to an
urban district; and

     (2)  Transfer lands reclassified under
paragraph (1) to the department of Hawaiian home lands or office of Hawaiian
affairs.

     (b)  The value of any land transferred, pursuant
to subsection (a), to the office of Hawaiian affairs shall be credited against
the pro rata portion of funds derived from the public land trust dedicated for
use by the office under sections 10-3 and 10-13.5.

     (c)  For the purposes of this
section, the value of the transferred land shall be determined after the land
has been reclassified for urban use."

     SECTION 2.  New statutory material is
underscored.

     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its
approval.

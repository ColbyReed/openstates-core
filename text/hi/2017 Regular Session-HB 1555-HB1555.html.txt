

     SECTION 1.  The director of finance is
authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $22,750,000 or so
much thereof as may be necessary, and the same sum or so much thereof as may be
necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2017-2018 for the purpose of capital
improvement projects for the forty-third representative district, as follows:

1.   To be
expended by the department of education:

     A.  Nanakuli high and intermediate school, Oahu

         Plans, design, and construction 

         for performing arts building and 

         theater.

                    Total funding               $ 1,750,000

2.   To be expended by the department of
transportation:

     A.  Kalaeloa airport, Oahu

         Plans, design, and construction 

         for utility system improvements.

                    Total funding               $10,000,000

     B.  Kalaeloa airport, Oahu

         Plans, design, and construction 

         for T-hanger construction.

                    Total funding               $
8,000,000

3.   To
be expended by the Hawaii community development authority: 

     A.  Kalaeloa, Oahu

         Plans, design and construction

         for the enterprise avenue

         energy corridor to midway road.

                    Total funding               $ 3,000,000

     SECTION 2.  The appropriations made for the capital
improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the
fiscal year for which the appropriation is made; provided that from the
appropriations made for fiscal year 2017-2018, all moneys unencumbered as of
June 30, 2020, shall lapse as of that date.

     SECTION 3.  The sums appropriated for the
respective capital improvement projects set forth in section 1 shall be
expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.

     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July
1, 2017.

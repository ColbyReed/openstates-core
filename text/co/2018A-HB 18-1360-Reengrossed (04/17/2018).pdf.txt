CONCERNING THE EXPANSION OF THE NUMBER OF DIRECTORS ON THE
BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF THE STATE HISTORICAL SOCIETY.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Colorado:
SECTION 1. In Colorado Revised Statutes, 24-80-201.5, amend
(1), (2)(a), (2)(b), and (2)(c); and repeal (4) as follows:
24-80-201.5. State historical society - board - appointment -
powers and duties. (1) Effective July 1, 2015 OCTOBER 1, 2018, the
board of directors of the state historical society, referred to in this part 2
as the "board" or "board of directors", consists of nine THIRTEEN members
appointed pursuant to subsection (2) of this section.
(2) (a) The governor shall appoint, with the consent of the senate,
nine THIRTEEN members of the board. If there is a vacancy of a member
appointed pursuant to this subsection (2)(a), the governor shall appoint a
new member of the board for the remainder of the unexpired term.
(b)   Except as otherwise provided in this subsection (2)(b),
members of the board serve three-year terms. The members first
appointed to the board take office on July 1, 2015. Of the appointments
of members to take office on July 1, 2015 FILL THE FOUR NEW POSITIONS
CREATED ON OCTOBER 1, 2018,       the governor shall designate:
(I) Three members ONE MEMBER of the board to have terms              A
TERM   expiring on July 1, 2016 JULY 1, 2019;
(II) Three members ONE MEMBER of the board to have terms A
TERM   expiring on July 1, 2017 JULY 1, 2020; and
(III) Three TWO members of the board to have terms expiring on
July 1, 2018 JULY 1, 2021. Thereafter, the terms of the members of the
board are three years.
(c) In appointing the nine THIRTEEN members of the board, the
governor shall ensure that collectively the members possess the skills in
state financial, legal, and regulatory expertise as required to oversee the
state historical society and shall consider both geographic and cultural
diversity of the nine THIRTEEN members. The governor shall appoint no
more than five SEVEN members from the same MAJOR OR MINOR political
party.
(4) Policies, procedures, bylaws, resolutions, and agreements
approved by the board prior to July 1, 2015, remain in full force and
effect unless and until changed by the board taking office July 1, 2015.
SECTION 2. Act subject to petition - effective date. This act
takes effect October 1, 2018; except that, if a referendum petition is filed
pursuant to section 1 (3) of article V of the state constitution against this
act or an item, section, or part of this act within the ninety-day period
after final adjournment of the general assembly, then the act, item,
section, or part will not take effect unless approved by the people at the
general election to be held in November 2018 and, in such case, will take
effect on the date of the official declaration of the vote thereon by the
governor.

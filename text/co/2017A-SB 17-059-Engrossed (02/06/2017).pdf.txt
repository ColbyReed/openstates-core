CONCERNING AN EXEMPTION FROM THE RULE REQUIRING A VEHICLE
TO SIGNAL AN INTENTION TO LEAVE THE CURRENT LANE OF
TRAVEL WHEN USING A ROUNDABOUT.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Colorado:
SECTION 1. In Colorado Revised Statutes, 42-1-102, amend the
introductory portion; and add (85.5) as follows:
42-1-102. Definitions. As used in articles 1 to 4 of this title TITLE
42, unless the context otherwise requires:
(85.5) "ROUNDABOUT" MEANS A CIRCULAR INTERSECTION WHERE
ROAD TRAFFIC SLOWS AND FLOWS IN ONE DIRECTION AROUND A CENTRAL
ISLAND BEFORE EXITING ONTO ONE OF THE CONNECTING ROADS.

SECTION 3.          Act subject to petition - effective date -
applicability. (1) This act takes effect at 12:01 a.m. on the day following
the expiration of the ninety-day period after final adjournment of the

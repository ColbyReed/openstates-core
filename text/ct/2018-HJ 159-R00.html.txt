RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THE HONORABLE ANTHONY P. FUSCO OF BROOKFIELD TO BE A FAMILY SUPPORT MAGISTRATE.
Resolved by this Assembly: 
That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the Honorable Anthony P. Fusco of Brookfield to be a Family Support Magistrate, to serve for a term of five years from the date of confirmation by both Houses, is approved and confirmed, and he is appointed a Family Support Magistrate for said term.

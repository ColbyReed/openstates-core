RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THE HONORABLE EDWARD F. STODOLINK OF STRATFORD TO BE A STATE REFEREE.
Resolved by this Assembly: 
That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the Honorable Edward F. Stodolink of Stratford to be a State Referee, to serve a term of eight years from July 1, 2018, is approved and confirmed, and he is appointed a State Referee for said term.

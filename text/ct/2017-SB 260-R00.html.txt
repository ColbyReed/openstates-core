AN ACT STUDYING AUTONOMOUS VEHICLES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That a task force be established to study autonomous vehicles and analyze how such vehicles will impact the automobile industry and the state. 
Statement of Purpose:  
To study autonomous vehicles.

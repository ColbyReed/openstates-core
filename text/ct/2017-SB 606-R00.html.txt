AN ACT CONCERNING JOURNEYMEN AND APPRENTICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That section 20-332b of the general statutes be amended to increase the allowable hiring ratio regarding apprentices to five apprentices for every one journeyman.
Statement of Purpose:  
To increase apprenticeship ratios.

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CREDIT AGAINST THE PERSONAL INCOME TAX FOR INTEREST PAID ON STUDENT LOANS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to establish a credit against the personal income tax for interest paid on student loans.
Statement of Purpose:  
To establish a credit against the personal income tax for interest paid on student loans.

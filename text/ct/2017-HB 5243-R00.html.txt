AN ACT REPEALING THE BUSINESS ENTITY TAX.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That section 12-284b of the general statutes be repealed, effective July 1, 2017, in order to eliminate the business entity tax.
Statement of Purpose:  
To repeal the business entity tax.

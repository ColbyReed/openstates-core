AN ACT STUDYING THE FEASIBILITY OF ESTABLISHING A STATE, FEDERAL AND PRIVATE PARTNERSHIP TO FUND A NEW DOCK FACILITY AT THE NAUTILUS MUSEUM IN GROTON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That the general statutes be amended to require a study of the feasibility of establishing a state, federal and private partnership to fund a new dock at the Nautilus Museum in Groton.  
Statement of Purpose:  
To study the feasibility of establishing a partnership to fund a new dock at the Nautilus Museum in Groton.

AN ACT REPLACING THE EXISTING COMMISSIONER'S NETWORK OF SCHOOLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That section 10-223h of the general statutes be amended to phase-out the existing commissioner's network of schools program and replace it with a new commissioner's network of schools program.
Statement of Purpose:  
To create an improved commissioner's network of schools.

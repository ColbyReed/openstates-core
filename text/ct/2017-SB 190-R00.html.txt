AN ACT CONCERNING AUCTION HOUSE TRANSACTION FEES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That the general statutes be amended to require auction houses to post or make available their transaction fees prior to auction.
Statement of Purpose:  
To provide full disclosure of all transaction fees prior to an auction.

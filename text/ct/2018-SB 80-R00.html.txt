AN ACT COMBATING THE OPIOID EPIDEMIC THROUGH INCREASED FUNDING FOR OPIOID ADDICTION TREATMENT CENTERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That the general statutes be amended to expand access to and increase funding for treatment facilities for opioid addiction
Statement of Purpose:  
To combat the opioid epidemic.

AN ACT PERMITTING BOARDS OF EDUCATION TO CHOOSE THE DATE OF THE FIRST DAY OF THE SCHOOL YEAR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That section 10-66q of the general statutes be amended to permit a local or regional board of education to choose the date for the start of school sessions.
Statement of Purpose:  
To allow boards of education to choose the start date of the school year.

The people of the State of California do enact as follows:SECTION 1. Section 13012.7 is added to the Penal Code, to read:13012.7. (a) The annual report published by the department pursuant to Section 13010 shall include statistics on child molestation in the same format and within the existing Table 1 and Table 2 that report on the number, rate per 100,000 population, and percentage change in other violent crimes, including rape.(b) For purposes of this section, child molestation shall include the combined total of offenses defined in subdivisions (a) and (b) of Section 288 and Section 288.5.

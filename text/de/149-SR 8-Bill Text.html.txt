
      
        BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the 149th General Assembly of the State of Delaware that Senate Resolution No. 2 relating to the Rules of the Delaware State Senate be amended by inserting after line 214 and before line 215 “(17) Veterans Affairs.”.
      
      
         
      
    

      
        SYNOPSIS
      
      
        This Resolution adds the Veterans Affairs Committee to the list of standing committees contained in Senate Rule 19 of Senate Resolution No. 2.


      
        WHEREAS
        ,
         teachers mold our future citizens through guidance and education; and
      
      
        WHEREAS, teachers create an atmosphere for children to grow and learn, and they inspire creativity and motivate their students; and
      
      
        WHEREAS, teachers encounter students of widely differing backgrounds and fill many roles in the lives of those students; and
      
      
        WHEREAS, our country’s future depends upon providing quality education to all students; and
      
      
        WHEREAS, teachers spend countless hours preparing lessons, evaluating progress, counseling and coaching students and performing community service; and
      
      
        WHEREAS, we recognize and support our teachers in educating the children of this State.
      
      
        NOW, THEREFORE:
      
      
        BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 149th General Assembly of the State of Delaware, the Senate concurring therein, that in celebration of and in gratitude for the work of the teachers in our State, we hereby recognize the week of May 8-12, 2017 as “Teacher Appreciation Week”.
      
      
         
      
    

      
        SYNOPSIS
      
      
        This resolution recognizes the week of May 8-12, 2017 as “Teacher Appreciation Week”.

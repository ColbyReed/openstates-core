An Act relating to the criminal offense of child endangerment
by prohibiting a sex offender access to a child or certain
minors, and providing penalties.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA:
Section 1. Section 726.6, Code 2018, is amended by adding
the following new subsection:
NEW SUBSECTION. 1A. A person who knowingly takes custody
or control over a child or minor, or who knowingly has
unsupervised access to a child or a minor while the person is
required to register as a sex offender under chapter 692A,
commits child endangerment. However, this subsection does not
apply to a person who is a parent or guardian of a child or a
minor who is required to register as a sex offender, or to a
person who is married to and living with a person required to
register as a sex offender.

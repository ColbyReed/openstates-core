

Be it
enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:

Section 1.  Title 33, chapter 2, article 4, Arizona
Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 33-277, to read:

START_STATUTE33-277.  Easement
holder; in lieu payments

A.  a
governmental entity that is the holder of a conservation easement shall make an
annual payment of monies in LIEU of property taxes.  The payments in lieu of
property taxes shall be paid to the county treasurer of the county in which the
real property burdened by the easement is located and shall be in the amount
that would have been assessed and collectable against the easement holder if
the easement holder had not been a governmental entity.

B.  Payments
made pursuant to this section shall be distributed by the county treasurer to
the county and to school districts, cities and towns in the county in the same
proportion as if the payments had been made by a private nongovernmental
conservation easement holder.END_STATUTE

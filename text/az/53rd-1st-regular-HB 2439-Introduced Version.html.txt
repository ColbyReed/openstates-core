

Be it
enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:

Section 1.  Section 36-425.01, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:

START_STATUTE36-425.01.  Home
health agencies; licensure; definition

A.  The
department by rule shall license home health agencies.  The
department shall prescribe a licensure period that does not exceed two years.

B.  In
lieu of a state licensure survey, the department shall issue a license to a
home health agency that submits an application prescribed by the department and
that meets the following requirements:

1.  Is
certified as a home health agency by medicare.

2.  Is
licensed by the department for at least one year.

3.  Meets
all department licensure requirements.

C.  The
department shall retain full authority over licensed home health agencies to
ensure that licensure requirements are maintained.

D.  A
licensed home health agency may provide home health services in this state by a
home health aide only if the home health aide meets the qualifications
specified in 42 Code of Federal Regulations section 484.36.

D. E.  For the purposes of
this section, "home health agency" has the same meaning as prescribed in section 36â€‘151.
END_STATUTE

A bill to be entitled
An act relating to the Appropriations Project titled
Tom P. Haney Technical Center--Industry Pipefitting
Request; providing an appropriation; providing an
effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
Section 1.       Tom P. Haney Technical Center--Industry
Pipefitting Request is an Appropriations Project as defined in
The Rules of The Florida House of Representatives and is
described in Appropriations Project Request 121, herein
incorporated by reference.
Section 2.       For fiscal year 2018-2019 the nonrecurring sum
of $785,600 from the General Revenue Fund is appropriated to the
Department of Education to fund the Tom P. Haney Technical
Center--Industry Pipefitting Request as described in
Appropriations Project Request 121.
Section 3.       This act shall take effect July 1, 2018.

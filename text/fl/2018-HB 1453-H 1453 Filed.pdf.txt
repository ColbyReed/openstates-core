A bill to be entitled
An act relating to Duval County; providing an
exception to general law; requiring the office of the
sheriff to provide crossing guards at certain schools;
providing an effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
Section 1.       Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary,
the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office shall provide crossing guards
for each school serving students in some or all of grades
kindergarten through grade 8 in Duval County.
Section 2.       This act shall take effect upon becoming a law.

A bill to be entitled
An act relating to the Appropriations Project titled
City of Bunnell Road Improvements; providing an
appropriation; providing an effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
Section 1.       City of Bunnell Road Improvements is an
Appropriations Project as defined in The Rules of The Florida
House of Representatives and is described in Appropriations
Project Request 944, herein incorporated by reference.
Section 2.       For fiscal year 2018-2019 the nonrecurring sum
of $450,000 from the State Transportation (Primary) Trust Fund
is appropriated to the Department of Transportation to fund the
City of Bunnell Road Improvements as described in Appropriations
Project Request 944.
Section 3.       This act shall take effect July 1, 2018.

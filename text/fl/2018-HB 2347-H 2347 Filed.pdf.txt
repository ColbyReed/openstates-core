A bill to be entitled
An act relating to the Appropriations Project titled
North Miami Foundation for Senior Citizens Services,
Inc. - Home Delivered Meals; providing an
appropriation; providing an effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
Section 1.       North Miami Foundation for Senior Citizens
Services, Inc. - Home Delivered Meals is an Appropriations
Project as defined in The Rules of The Florida House of
Representatives and is described in Appropriations Project
Request 209, herein incorporated by reference.
Section 2.       For fiscal year 2018-2019 the nonrecurring sum
of $250,000 from the General Revenue Fund is appropriated to the
Department of Elder Affairs to fund the North Miami Foundation
for Senior Citizens Services, Inc. - Home Delivered Meals as
described in Appropriations Project Request 209.
Section 3.       This act shall take effect July 1, 2018.

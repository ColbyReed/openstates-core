A bill to be entitled
An act relating to the Appropriations Project titled
Ocala Silver Springs Stormwater and Nutrient Reduction
Project; providing an appropriation; providing an
effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
Section 1.       Ocala Silver Springs Stormwater and Nutrient
Reduction Project is an Appropriations Project as defined in The
Rules of The Florida House of Representatives and is described
in Appropriations Project Request 264, herein incorporated by
reference.
Section 2.       For fiscal year 2018-2019 the nonrecurring sum
of $300,000 from the General Revenue Fund is appropriated to the
Department of Environmental Protection to fund the Ocala Silver
Springs Stormwater and Nutrient Reduction Project as described
in Appropriations Project Request 264.
Section 3.       This act shall take effect July 1, 2018.

A bill to be entitled
An act relating to the Appropriations Project titled
Arc Broward - Equipment and Generators for Facilities
Serving the Developmentally Disabled; providing an
appropriation; providing an effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
Section 1.       Arc Broward - Equipment and Generators for
Facilities Serving the Developmentally Disabled is an
Appropriations Project as defined in The Rules of The Florida
House of Representatives and is described in Appropriations
Project Request 721, herein incorporated by reference.
Section 2.       For fiscal year 2018-2019 the nonrecurring sum
of $950,000 from the General Revenue Fund is appropriated to the
Agency for Persons with Disabilities to fund the Arc Broward -
Equipment and Generators for Facilities Serving the
Developmentally Disabled as described in Appropriations Project
Request 721.
Section 3.       This act shall take effect July 1, 2018.

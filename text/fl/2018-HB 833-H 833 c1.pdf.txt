A bill to be entitled
An act relating to retail theft; amending s. 921.0022,
F.S.; increasing the offense level on the offense
severity ranking chart of the Criminal Punishment Code
for petit theft third or subsequent offense, third-
degree felony communications fraud of specified value,
and second-degree communications fraud of specified
value; providing an effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
Section 1.          Paragraphs (a), (c), (d), (e), and (f) of
subsection (3) of section 921.0022, Florida Statutes, are
amended to read:
921.0022          Criminal Punishment Code; offense severity
ranking chart.--
(3)     OFFENSE SEVERITY RANKING CHART
(a)     LEVEL 1
(c)     LEVEL 3
(d)     LEVEL 4
(e)     LEVEL 5
(f)     LEVEL 6
years of age or older.
Section 2.         This act shall take effect July 1, 2019.

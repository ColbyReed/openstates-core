


  

  
SENATE RESOLUTION

  
    
WHEREAS, The members of the Illinois Senate are saddened to 
learn of the death of William "Bill" Stuart Moser of Urbana, 
who passed away on January 5, 2018; and

  
    
WHEREAS, Bill Moser was born in Urbana to Steve and Nancy 
(Pepper) Moser on September 21, 1967; he graduated from St. 
Joseph-Ogden High School in 1985; he earned a Bachelor of 
Science in Physics and Astronomy from the University of 
Illinois; he then earned a Master's Degree in Astronomy from 
the University of Florida; and

  
    
WHEREAS, Bill Moser taught physics and astronomy at several 
community colleges; he then decided to return to his family's 
farm, which he had planned to take over when his parents 
retired; and

  
    
WHEREAS, Bill Moser enjoyed music, foreign languages, and  
working with and refurbishing computers; he will be dearly 
missed by those who loved him; and

  
    
WHEREAS, Bill Moser was preceded in death by his paternal 
grandfather, Wayne Moser; and his maternal grandparents, Alice 
and William Pepper; and

  
    
WHEREAS, Bill Moser is survived by his parents; his 
sisters, Anne (James) Shumar and Katie Moser (Nathan Hankins); 
his paternal grandmother, Helen Moser Jedlick; and his four 
nieces; therefore, be it

  
    
RESOLVED, BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDREDTH GENERAL 
ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ILLINOIS, that we mourn the passing of 
William "Bill" Stuart Moser and extend our sincere condolences 
to his family, friends, and all who knew and loved him; and be 
it further

  
    
RESOLVED, That a suitable copy of this resolution be 
presented to the family of Bill Moser as an expression of our 
deepest sympathy.

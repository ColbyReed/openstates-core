Sec. 1-2. 
Construction. 
The
 
The
 provisions of this Act, so 
far as they are the same as those of any
prior statute, shall 
be construed as a continuation of such prior
provisions, and 
not as a new enactment.


    
If in any other statute reference is made to an Act of the 
General
Assembly, or a section of such an Act, which is 
continued in this School
Code, such reference shall be held to 
refer to the Act or section thereof
so continued in this Code.


(Source: Laws 1961, p. 31
.)

"[AGENCY REPORTING TO LEGISLATURE]
Section 333.      Each agency receiving a general fund
appropriation in this act shall, prior to submitting any report
in hard copy to the legislature or any committee or task force
thereof, notify the management council of the legislature of any
report estimated to cost one hundred dollars ($100.00) or more
to print.   Unless objected to by management council after five
(5) days from the notice, the agency shall submit the report
electronically when due.     Nothing in this section shall be
construed to require an agency to print in hard copy, except as
otherwise provided by Wyoming law or policy of the management
council.".

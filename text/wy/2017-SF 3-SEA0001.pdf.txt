AN ACT relating to the select water committee; specifying
that the select water committee shall review projects and
make recommendations as specified; and providing for an
effective date.

Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:

     Section 1.   W.S. 28-11-101(b)(v) is amended to read:

     28-11-101. Appointment of members; powers and duties;
related duties of water development commission.

     (b)   The select committee shall:

          (v) Review the budgets of all projects and make
recommendations regardless of funding source.

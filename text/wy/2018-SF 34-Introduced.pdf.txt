Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
Section 1.       W.S. 27-3-311(a)(i)(D) is amended to read:
27-3-311.     Disqualifications                  from      entitlement;
grounds; forfeiture.
(a)    An individual shall be disqualified from benefit
entitlement      beginning         with       the    effective    date    of   an
otherwise valid claim or the week during which the failure
occurred,       until         he     has        been      employed       in    an

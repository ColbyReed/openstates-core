AN ACT relating to education; making permissive the
requirement that school districts provide foreign language
instruction to students in kindergarten through second
grade; and providing for an effective date.

Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:

     Section 1.   W.S. 21-9-101(g) is amended to read:

     21-9-101. Educational      programs    for     schools;
standards; core of knowledge and skills; special needs
programs; class size requirements; cocurricular activities.

     (g) Not later than the 2002-2003 school year, all Any
school districts shall district may provide instruction in
foreign languages to students in kindergarten through grade
2. A school district that provides instruction in a foreign
language pursuant to this subsection shall do so in
accordance with standards promulgated by the state board.

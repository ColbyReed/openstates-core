- 2018 LEGISLATURE
AN ACT to create 118.125 (2) (dm) of the statutes; relating to: allowing a school
board to share safety camera footage with law enforcement.
SECTION 1. 118.125 (2) (dm) of the statutes is created to read:
118.125 (2) (dm) Safety camera footage may be made available to a law
enforcement agency to the extent the school board determines sharing the footage
with the law enforcement agency serves a legitimate safety interest.
(END)

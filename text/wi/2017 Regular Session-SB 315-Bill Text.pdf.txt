SENATE BILL 315
AN ACT relating to: a study of affordable housing financing.
SECTION 1.0Nonstatutory provisions.
(1) STUDY OF FUNDING FOR CREATING NEW AFFORDABLE HOUSING. The Wisconsin
Housing and Economic Development Authority shall conduct a study of funding
mechanisms for creating affordable housing for low-income persons in this state.
The study shall include an evaluation of social impact bonding and other bonding
programs for creating affordable housing units. No later than January 31, 2018, the

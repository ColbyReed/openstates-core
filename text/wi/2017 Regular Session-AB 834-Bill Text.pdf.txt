- 2018 LEGISLATURE
ASSEMBLY BILL 834
AN ACT to amend 446.02 (3) (b) of the statutes; relating to: the score required
for successful completion of chiropractic examinations.
SECTION 1. 446.02 (3) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:
446.02 (3) (b) Parts I, II, III, and IV of the examination administered by the
National Board of Chiropractic Examiners. An applicant successfully completes
Part III of that examination if the applicant scores at least 438 375 on that part. An
applicant successfully completes Part IV of that examination if the applicant scores
at least 475 375 on that part.

SECTION 2.0Initial applicability.
(1)   This act first applies to an application for a license to practice as a
chiropractor that the department of safety and professional services receives on the
effective date of this subsection.

SECTION 2. 20.455 (2) (en) of the statutes is created to read:
20.455 (2) (en) Diversion pilot program. The amounts in the schedule to create
a diversion pilot program for nonviolent offenders to be diverted to a treatment
option.
SECTION 3. 20.455 (2) (en) of the statutes, as created by 2017 Wisconsin Act ....
(this act), is repealed.
SECTION 4.0Nonstatutory provisions.
(1) DIVERSION PILOT PROGRAM. The department of justice shall submit to the joint
committee on finance a request to supplement the appropriation under section
20.455 (2) (en) of the statutes with the funds that are held in the appropriation
account under section 20.865 (4) (a) of the statutes for the purpose described in
SECTION 5 (3) of this act along with a proposed plan for the expenditure of the moneys.
If the cochairpersons of the committee do not notify the department of justice within
14 working days after the date of the department's submittal that the committee has
scheduled a meeting for the purpose of reviewing the proposed plan, the joint
committee on finance shall release the funds and the department of justice may
expend the moneys to implement the proposed plan. If, within 14 working days after
the date of the department's submittal, the cochairpersons of the committee notify
the department of justice that the committee has scheduled a meeting for the purpose
of reviewing the proposed plan, the department of justice may expend the moneys

SECTION 5.0Fiscal changes.
(1) TREATMENT AND DIVERSION PROGRAM GRANTS. In the schedule under section
20.005 (3) of the statutes for the appropriation to the department of justice under
section 20.455 (2) (em) of the statutes, the dollar amount for fiscal year 2017-18 is
increased by $2,000,000 for the purpose for which the appropriation is made. In the
schedule under section 20.005 (3) of the statutes for the appropriation to the
department of justice under section 20.455 (2) (em) of the statutes, the dollar amount
for fiscal year 2018-19 is increased by $2,000,000 for the purpose for which the
appropriation is made.
(2) TREATMENT     AND DIVERSION PROGRAMS EXPANSION.       In the schedule under
section 20.005 (3) of the statutes for the appropriation to the department of justice
under section 20.455 (2) (em) of the statutes, the dollar amount for fiscal year
2017-18 is increased by $150,000 for the expansion of the grant program under
section 165.95 (2) of the statutes to additional counties. In the schedule under section
20.005 (3) of the statutes for the appropriation to the department of justice under
section 20.455 (2) (em) of the statutes, the dollar amount for fiscal year 2018-19 is
increased by $150,000 for the expansion of the grant program under section 165.95
(2) of the statutes to additional counties.

statutes for the appropriation to the joint committee on finance under section 20.865

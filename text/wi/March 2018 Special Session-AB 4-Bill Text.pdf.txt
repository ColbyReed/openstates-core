- 2018 LEGISLATURE
AN ACT to amend 118.46 (1) (intro.) and 118.46 (1) (a) 5. of the statutes; relating
to: the Department of Public Instruction's model school policy on bullying by
pupils.
SECTION 1. 118.46 (1) (intro.) of the statutes is amended to read:
118.46 (1) (intro.) By March 1, 2010, the The department shall do all of the
following:
SECTION 2. 118.46 (1) (a) 5. of the statutes is amended to read:
- 2018 Legislature              -2-                                 LRB-5683/1
118.46 (1) (a) 5.    A procedure for investigating reports of bullying.    The
procedure shall identify the school district employee in each school who is
responsible for conducting the investigation and require that the parent or guardian
of each pupil involved in a bullying incident be notified within 48 hours of the
incident being reported to a school district employee.
SECTION 3.0Nonstatutory provisions.
(1) MODEL   POLICY ON BULLYING.   The department of public instruction shall
revise its model school policy on bullying under section 118.46 of the statutes to
comply with this act by August 1, 2018.
(END)

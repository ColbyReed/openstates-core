SECTION 3. 115.28 (63) of the statutes is created to read:
115.28 (63) MENTAL    HEALTH TRAINING PROGRAM.      Establish a mental health
training support program under which the department provides training on the
screening, brief intervention, and referral to treatment program, an evidence-based
strategy related to addressing mental health issues in schools, to school district staff
and instructional staff of charter schools under s. 118.40 (2r) or (2x).

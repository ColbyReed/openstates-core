SENATE BILL 451
AN ACT to create 100.20 (2) (c) of the statutes; relating to: the sale or rental of
soda water beverage equipment.
SECTION 1. 100.20 (2) (c) of the statutes is created to read:
100.20 (2) (c) Notwithstanding par. (a), the department may not issue any order
or promulgate any rule that regulates the terms of sale or rental of equipment for

as defined in s. 97.34 (1) (b), other than coin-operated vending machines.

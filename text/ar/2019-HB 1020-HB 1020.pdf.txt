                                              Subtitle
                        TO ALLOW OUT-OF-STATE TEACHING EXPERIENCE
                        TO BE CONSIDERED WHEN DETERMINING A
                        TEACHER'S SALARY SCHEDULE.




BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:


       SECTION 1.       Arkansas Code § 6-17-2403(c)(3), concerning the minimum
teacher compensation schedule, is amended to read as follows:
               (3)      For purposes of this section, “years of service experience”
means:
                        (A)   Performing the full-time duties of a teacher for a
full school year with a valid Arkansas teaching license;
                        (B)   Years of employment with an Arkansas public school in
a full-time position that requires that the teacher have an Arkansas teaching
license; or
                        (C)   Years of employment in an educational capacity with an
institution in subdivision (c)(1)(C) of this section with a valid Arkansas
teaching license.
               (4)(A)       An Arkansas public school may adopt a policy that
recognizes the years of experience of an out-of-state teacher when
determining the teacher's placement on the salary schedule of the public

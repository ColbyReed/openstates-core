BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:


       SECTION 1.       Arkansas Code § 6-17-2403(c), concerning the minimum
teacher compensation schedule, is amended to read as follows:
       (c)(1)       For purposes of the salary schedules described in this section,
a school district may consider the following:
                        (A)   the The teacher's experience is his or her total
years' experience as a teacher with a valid Arkansas teaching license and
teaching at any:
                        (A)(i)    Public school accredited by the Department of
Education or a nationally recognized accrediting association;
                        (B)(ii)    Private school within the State of Arkansas
accredited by a nationally recognized accrediting association;
                        (C)(iii)     Institution of higher education within the State
of Arkansas accredited by a nationally recognized higher education

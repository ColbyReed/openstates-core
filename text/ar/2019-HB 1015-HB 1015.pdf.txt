BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:


       SECTION 1.       Arkansas Code Title 6, Chapter 16, Subchapter 1, is amended
to add an additional section to read as follows:
       6-16-151.        Journalism course required to be taught.
       (a)    A public school that serves students in grades nine through twelve
(9-12) shall teach a journalism course each school year.
       (b)    The State Board of Education shall include a journalism course in
the list of courses required to be taught at schools that serve students in
grades nine through twelve (9-12).

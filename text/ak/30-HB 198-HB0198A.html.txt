HOUSE BILL NO. 198                                                                          
"An Act relating to per diem for members of the legislature."                                                           
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
* Section 1. AS 24.10.130(b) is amended to read:                                                                   
(b)  Legislators and officers and employees of the legislative branch of                                                
government may be entitled to a per diem allowance. A per diem allowance for a                                      
legislator may not exceed the applicable per diem allowance rate in effect for                                      
state employees in the general government unit.                                                               
* Sec. 2. AS 39.23 is amended by adding a new section to read:                                                     
Sec. 39.23.555. Recommendations relating to allowances. The                                                
recommendations of the commission relating to allowances may not include a                                        
recommendation that a member of the legislature receive a per diem allowance at a                                 
rate greater than the rate allowed under AS 24.10.130(b).

HOUSE BILL NO. 122                                                                           
"An Act relating to the introduction of bills."                                                                         
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
* Section 1. AS 24.08.060(a) is amended to read:                                                                   
(a)  A member of the legislature or a committee chair, after consultation with                                      
all of the active members of a committee and [CHAIRMAN,] with the concurrence                                       
of a majority of the active members of the committee and on behalf of the committee,                                    
may introduce a bill or resolution. Bills and resolutions shall be prepared and                                         
introduced in the manner and form prescribed in the uniform rules and the legislative                                   
style manual.

A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR ADJOURNMENT SINE DIE OF THE 2018
THIRD EXTRA SESSION.
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
SECTION 1. When the House of Representatives and the Senate, constituting the
2018 Third Extra Session of the General Assembly, do adjourn on Monday, October 15, 2018,
they stand adjourned sine die.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.

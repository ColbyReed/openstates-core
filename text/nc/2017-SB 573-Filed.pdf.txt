 2   AN ACT TO MODIFY THE LAW RELATED TO APPRAISAL ASSIGNMENTS.
 3   The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
 4              SECTION 1. G.S. 93E-2-4(i) reads as rewritten:
 5       "(i)   For appraisal assignments of property secured by the principal dwelling of the
 6   consumer, one- to four-family residential dwellings, an appraisal management company shall
 7   compensate appraisers in compliance with section 129E(i) of the federal Truth in Lending Act
 8   (15 U.S.C. ยง 1601 et seq.) and regulations promulgated thereunder. The Board shall adopt rules
 9   necessary to enforce this subsection. Rules establishing customary and reasonable rates shall be
10   based on objective third-party information, such as academic studies and independent private
11   sector surveys."
12              SECTION 2. This act becomes effective October 1, 2017.

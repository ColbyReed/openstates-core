A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT TO REQUIRE THAT THE BURDEN OF PROOF IN CERTAIN CHALLENGES TO
CANDIDACY BE PLACED ON THE CHALLENGER UNDER THE LAWS
GOVERNING ELECTIONS.
The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
SECTION 1. G.S. 163A-1029(a) reads as rewritten:
"(a) The Except for a challenge based on residency as provided in subsection (b) of this
section, the burden of proof shall be upon the candidate,challenger, who must show by a
preponderance of the evidence of the record as a whole that he or she isthe candidate is not
qualified to be a candidate for the office."
SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to challenges to
candidacy on or after that date.

 1                                      A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 2   AN ACT TO REQUIRE THAT THE BURDEN OF PROOF IN CERTAIN CHALLENGES TO
 3       CANDIDACY BE PLACED ON THE CHALLENGER UNDER THE LAWS
 4       GOVERNING ELECTIONS.
 5   The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
 6               SECTION 1. G.S. 163A-1029(a) reads as rewritten:
 7       "(a) The Except for a challenge based on residency as provided in subsection (b) of this
 8   section, the burden of proof shall be upon the candidate,challenger, who must show by a
 9   preponderance of the evidence of the record as a whole that he or she isthe candidate is not
10   qualified to be a candidate for the office."
11               SECTION 2. This act is effective when it becomes law and applies to challenges to
12   candidacy on or after that date.

 1   A HOUSE RESOLUTION ADOPTING THE PERMANENT RULES OF THE 2018 FIRST
 2       EXTRA SESSION OF THE NORTH CAROLINA HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES.
 3   Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:
 4               SECTION 1. The permanent rules of the 2017 Regular Session of the House of
 5   Representatives, with the following amendments, are adopted as the permanent rules of the 2018
 6   First Extra Session:
 7               (1)    RULE 1 shall read as follows: "RULE 1. Convening Hour. The House shall
 8                      convene each legislative day at the hour fixed by the House. In the event the
 9                      House adjourns on the preceding legislative day without having fixed an hour
10                      for reconvening, the House shall convene on the next legislative day at 10:00
11                      A.M."
12               (2)    Notwithstanding RULE 26, the Committee on Rules, Calendar, and
13                      Operations of the House, the Ethics Committee, the Judiciary I, II, III, and IV
14                      Committees, the Finance Committee, and the Appropriations Committee (not
15                      including the Appropriations Committees for budget specific areas) shall be
16                      comprised of the members who were appointed during the Regular Session
17                      and other members as the Speaker may appoint at the beginning of the First
18                      Extra Session. The Chairs and Vice-Chairs of those committees shall be those
19                      who were appointed by the Speaker during the Regular Session.
20               (3)    Notwithstanding RULE 27, the standing committees of the House are:
21                              Committees
22                      a.      Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
23                      b.      Ethics
24                      c.      Judiciary I
25                      d.      Judiciary II
26                      e.      Judiciary III
27                      f.      Judiciary IV
28                      g.      Finance
29                      h.      Appropriations
30                      All rules of the 2017 Regular Session applicable to standing committees of
31                      the House other than the committees named in this subdivision are
32                      inoperative. The Speaker may appoint select committees and fill vacancies of
33                      standing committees under RULE 26.
34               (4)    Notwithstanding RULE 31(a), any bill or resolution shall be introduced by
35                      submitting the bill or resolution to the Principal Clerk's Office.
36               (5)    Notwithstanding RULES 31 and 31.1:

 9                 legislative day of its introduction or receipt from the Senate.
10            (8)  Notwithstanding RULE 36(b) and (b1), a bill reported favorably by a

12                 calendar on the same day it is reported and a proposed committee substitute
13                 does not have to be distributed electronically to committee members prior to
14                 being considered by the committee.
15            (9)  Notwithstanding RULE 41:
16                 a.       The first reading and reference to a standing committee of a House bill
17                          shall occur on the legislative day of its introduction. The first reading
18                          and reference to a standing committee of a Senate bill shall occur on
19                          the legislative day of its receipt on messages from the Senate. The
20                          Speaker shall give notice at each subsequent reading whether it is the
21                          second or third reading.
22                 b.       Except for a bill governed by Section 23 of Article II of the North
23                          Carolina Constitution, a bill may be read more than once on the same
24                          day.
25            (10) Notwithstanding RULE 44(d), a conference report may be placed on the
26                 calendar for the legislative day on which the report is received.
27            (11) Notwithstanding RULE 44.2, a vote may be taken on overriding a
28                 gubernatorial veto on a House or Senate bill the same legislative day of notice
29                 of its placement on the calendar.
30            (12) Notwithstanding RULE 59, any member wishing to cosponsor a bill or
31                 resolution that has been introduced may do so on the day during which the bill
32                 or resolution was first read and referred, but only while the bill or resolution
33                 is in the possession of the House.
34            SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.

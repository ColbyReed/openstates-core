A JOINT RESOLUTION ADJOURNING THE 2018 THIRD EXTRA SESSION TO A DATE
CERTAIN.
Be it resolved by the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring:
SECTION 1. When the House of Representatives and the Senate jointly adjourn on
Tuesday, October 2, 2018, they stand adjourned to reconvene the 2018 Third Extra Session on
Monday, October 15, 2018, at 10:00 a.m.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.

 1   A HOUSE RESOLUTION ADOPTING THE PERMANENT RULES OF THE 2018 THIRD
 2       EXTRA SESSION OF THE NORTH CAROLINA HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES.
 3   Be it resolved by the House of Representatives:
 4               SECTION 1. The permanent rules of the 2017 Regular Session of the House of
 5   Representatives, with the following amendments, are adopted as the permanent rules of the 2018
 6   Third Extra Session:
 7               (1)    RULE 1 shall read as follows: "RULE 1. Convening Hour. The House shall
 8                      convene each legislative day at the hour fixed by the House. In the event the
 9                      House adjourns on the preceding legislative day without having fixed an hour
10                      for reconvening, the House shall convene on the next legislative day at 10:00
11                      A.M."
12               (2)    Notwithstanding RULE 26, the Committee on Rules, Calendar, and
13                      Operations of the House, the Ethics Committee, the Finance Committee, and
14                      the Appropriations Committee (not including the Appropriations Committees
15                      for budget-specific areas) shall be comprised of the members who were
16                      appointed during the Regular Session and other members as the Speaker may
17                      appoint at the beginning of the Third Extra Session. The Chairs and
18                      Vice-Chairs of those committees shall be those who were appointed by the
19                      Speaker during the Regular Session.
20               (3)    Notwithstanding RULE 27, the standing committees of the House are:
21                              Committees
22                      a.      Rules, Calendar, and Operations of the House
23                      b.      Ethics
24                      c.      Appropriations
25                      d.      Finance
26                      All rules of the 2017 Regular Session applicable to standing committees of
27                      the House other than the committees named in this subdivision are
28                      inoperative. The Speaker may appoint select committees and fill vacancies of
29                      standing committees under RULE 26.
30               (4)    Notwithstanding RULE 31(a), any bill or resolution shall be introduced by
31                      submitting the bill or resolution to the Principal Clerk's Office.
32               (5)    Notwithstanding RULES 31 and 31.1:
33                      a.      The only resolutions that may be introduced or considered in the
34                              House are resolutions adjourning the 2018 Third Extra Session sine
35                              die.
36                      b.      All bills shall be excluded from introduction or consideration in the
37                              House, other than bills addressing emergency management and

 1                         disaster recovery related to Hurricane Florence and resiliency planning
 2                         for future natural disasters.
 3            (6)  RULE 31.1 shall not apply to resolutions adjourning the 2018 Third Extra
 4                 Session sine die and bills addressing emergency management and disaster
 5                 recovery related to Hurricane Florence and resiliency planning for future
 6                 natural disasters.
 7            (7)  Notwithstanding RULES 32 and 41, a resolution or bill may be placed on the
 8                 calendar without being referred by the Speaker to a committee and on the same
 9                 legislative day of its introduction or receipt from the Senate.
10            (8)  Notwithstanding RULE 34(b), the Principal Clerk may accept a bill for
11                 introduction if the draft contains names preprinted on the bill jacket and body
12                 of the bill as primary sponsors without the signature of each member listed.
13            (9)  Notwithstanding RULE 36(b) and (b1), a bill reported favorably by a
14                 committee or received for concurrence may be placed on the favorable
15                 calendar on the same day it is reported and a proposed committee substitute
16                 does not have to be distributed electronically to committee members prior to
17                 being considered by the committee.
18            (10) Notwithstanding RULE 38(b), a bill that in any way reduces revenue or
19                 waives a fee may be reported to the Standing Committee on Appropriations
20                 instead of the Committee on Finance and does not need to be reported to the
21                 Committee on Finance.
22            (11) Notwithstanding RULE 41:
23                 a.      The first reading and reference to a standing committee of a House bill
24                         shall occur on the legislative day of its introduction. The first reading
25                         and reference to a standing committee of a Senate bill shall occur on
26                         the legislative day of its receipt on messages from the Senate. The
27                         Speaker shall give notice at each subsequent reading whether it is the
28                         second or third reading.
29                 b.      Except for a bill governed by Section 23 of Article II of the North
30                         Carolina Constitution, a bill may be read more than once on the same
31                         day.
32            (12) Notwithstanding RULE 44(d), a conference report may be placed on the
33                 calendar for the legislative day on which the report is received.
34            (13) Notwithstanding RULE 59:
35                 a.      These rules do not limit the number of members who may be listed as
36                         primary sponsors.
37                 b.      Any member wishing to cosponsor a bill or resolution that has been
38                         introduced may do so on the day during which the bill or resolution
39                         was first read and referred, but only while the bill or resolution is in
40                         the possession of the House.
41            SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon adoption.

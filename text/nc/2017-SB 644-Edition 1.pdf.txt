A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS OF THE
UNIVERSITY OF NORTH CAROLINA FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND
IMPLEMENTATION             OF     PHYSICIAN       ASSISTANT      CURRICULUM           AT
WINSTON-SALEM STATE UNIVERSITY AND FOR THE RECRUITMENT OF
STUDENTS FROM DIVERSE RACIAL AND ETHNIC BACKGROUNDS IN ORDER
TO INCREASE THE RACIAL AND ETHNIC DIVERSITY OF LICENSED PHYSICIAN
ASSISTANTS PRACTICING IN THIS STATE.
The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
SECTION 1. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Board of
Governors of The University of North Carolina the sum of ___ ($) for the 2017-2018 fiscal
year and the sum of ___ ($) for the 2018-2019 fiscal year. These funds shall be allocated to
Winston-Salem State University (WSSU) to do the following:
(1)   Develop and implement curriculum at WSSU for a physician assistant
degree program.
(2)   Develop strategies and recruitment methods for encouraging talented
students from diverse racial and ethnic backgrounds to enroll at WSSU in
the physician assistant degree program developed pursuant to subdivision (1)
of this section.
SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2017.

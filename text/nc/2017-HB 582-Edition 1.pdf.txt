 9   PART I. POLICY ON ABUSE AND NEGLECT RESOURCES
10               SECTION 1.(a) G.S. 115C-12 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
11               "(46) Duty Regarding Abuse and Neglect. – Upon consideration and
12                       recommendation by the Superintendent of Public Instruction, the State Board
13                       of Education shall adopt a policy to be implemented by local boards of
14                       education to address student awareness of child abuse and neglect, including
15                       sexual abuse. This policy shall include a requirement that the local boards of
16                       education provide students in grades six through 12 with a document at the
17                       beginning of each school year that provides (i) the telephone number used
18                       for reporting abuse to the department of social services in the county in
19                       which the local school administrative unit is located, in accordance with
20                       G.S. 7B-301, and (ii) information about the resources developed pursuant to
21                       G.S. 115C-105.51, including the anonymous safety tip line application."

39   PART III. APPROPRIATION
40              SECTION 3. There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Department of

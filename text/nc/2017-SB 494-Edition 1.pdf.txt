                                          SENATE BILL 494


    Short Title:   Ready Mix Concrete Mill Sales Tax Exemption.                                (Public)
    Sponsors:      Senator Gunn (Primary Sponsor).
    Referred to:   Rules and Operations of the Senate
                                             March 30, 2017

1                                      A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
2   AN ACT TO EXEMPT CERTAIN PARTS SALES FOR READY-MIX CONCRETE MILLS.
3   The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
4               SECTION 1. G.S. 105-164.13 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
5               "(5e) Sales to a company that primarily sells ready-mix concrete for repair or
6                       replacement parts for a ready-mix concrete mill that is freestanding or that is
7                       affixed to a motor vehicle."
8               SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2017, and applies to sales made on
9   or after that date.

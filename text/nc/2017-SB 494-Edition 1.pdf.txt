A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
AN ACT TO EXEMPT CERTAIN PARTS SALES FOR READY-MIX CONCRETE MILLS.
The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
SECTION 1. G.S. 105-164.13 is amended by adding a new subdivision to read:
"(5e) Sales to a company that primarily sells ready-mix concrete for repair or
replacement parts for a ready-mix concrete mill that is freestanding or that is
affixed to a motor vehicle."
SECTION 2. This act becomes effective July 1, 2017, and applies to sales made on
or after that date.

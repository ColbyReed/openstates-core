A JOINT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR ADJOURNMENT SINE DIE OF THE 2018
SECOND EXTRA SESSION.
Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring:
SECTION 1. When the House of Representatives and the Senate, constituting the
2018 Second Extra Session of the General Assembly, adjourn on Monday, August 27, 2018, they
stand adjourned sine die.
SECTION 2. This resolution is effective upon ratification.
